Agriculture Advisory and Extension Services

What Are Agricultural Advisory & Extension Services?

Agricultural Advisory and Extension Services (AAES) are systems that connect farmers with practical knowledge, tools, and support to improve productivity, sustainability, and income.

Core Functions

. Information & Knowledge Transfer
Deliver research-based guidance on crop production, livestock care, soil management, input use, and market trends.
.Technology Adoption Support
Show farmers how to use new seeds, machinery, irrigation techniques, or digital tools.
.Capacity Building
Training, workshops, field days, and demonstrations to build skills.
.Problem Diagnosis & Solutions
On-farm visits to identify pest/disease problems and recommend solutions.
.Market Linkages
Helping farmers access buyers, contractors, cooperatives, and value chains.
.Feedback Loop
Taking farmers' challenges back to researchers and policymakers.
